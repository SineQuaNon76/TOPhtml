This is Slow Cooker Sausage with Sauce

Ingredients

  • 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
  • 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
  • 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 6 hoagie rolls, split lengthwise (Optional)

Direction

  • Step 1 Gather all ingredients.
  • Step 2 Place Italian sausage links, spaghetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into a slow cooker; mix until well combined.
  • Step 3 Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls.
